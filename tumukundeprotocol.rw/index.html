A little Story About Us

Our History

Founded by TUMUKUNDE Anastasie in May 2022, Blessed Shop is your destination for quality footwear and fashion apparel tailored for both men and women. Our downtown location serves as the hub for our diverse collection of shoes and clothing. Meanwhile, our Remera branch, conveniently situated near Masterpiece, is the go-to spot for an exquisite selection of vibrant flowers, adding a touch of nature's beauty to your shopping experience.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with high-quality, stylish footwear that not only complements their individuality but also ensures comfort and durability. We aim to create a seamless shopping experience, fostering customer satisfaction and loyalty while maintaining a commitment to ethical and sustainable practices in our business operations.

Our Vision

We aspire to continuously expand our product range, embracing diversity in design and functionality. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices, we strive to be a global brand that not only meets but exceeds the expectations of our customers, setting new standards for excellence in the footwear industry
