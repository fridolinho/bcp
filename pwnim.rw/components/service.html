TRAINING: We provide training at all levels of the church: we train pastors, evangelists, apostles, prophets, teachers, singers, deacons, protocols ... we teach the bible, we give many different teachings about the work of God, in terms of church growth. We train and teach all levels of the church to stand up for their responsibilities.
